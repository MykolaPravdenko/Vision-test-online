Q AND A

What are Tumbling E eye chart tests?

A tumbling E chart uses the letter E rotated in different directions. It is used to test visual acuity for people who cannot read letters.

What does 2/10 or 2/2, 2/1.5 mean?

This is a reduced near-vision version of a standard chart. 2/10 means very poor eyesight, 2/2 good eyesight, 2/1.5 perfect eyesight.

What is myopia?

Nearsightedness (myopia) means close objects are clear, but distant objects appear blurry.

What is hyperopia?

Farsightedness (hyperopia) means distant objects are clearer than close objects.

What is astigmatism?

Astigmatism occurs when the eye has an irregular shape, causing blurred vision at all distances.
